1. I dont want to spend my day's writing more crud features.
2. A full enbrace of BDD as a fundamental source of truth for a repository

4. The 'shift left' mentality has forced teams to plan heavily before work finally.

### Cons
1. Trade off between speed and understanding.
    - most humans don't retain information just be reading once.
    - volume and size of PRs is too large.
2. When downtime is measured in seconds and dollars, who's responsible?
3. What happened to SMEs? Those don't exist in this new workflow.
4. Non-deterministic. Stop comparing c vs asm to ai coding vs us coding.
5. Are teasts just ceremoney?
--- Below are my biggest problems:
6. Erosion of open source. Licenses no longr matter, 
7. Fragmented open source and a move back to closed source.
8. What happens when altruism is gone? What happens when Anthropic is a public company? Ads and bias in the model's answers?
9. What happens when Claude (or another tools) locks you into an environment?

    - anthropic tooling that claude starts using that requires unlimited access to your code and business by claude.
